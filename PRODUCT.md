# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Office team operators and editors use this dashboard while managing long-running PROYA Indonesian skincare livestream VOD jobs. They monitor queue state, review generated clips, inspect scores and compliance flags, check module readiness, and manage rendered assets under production pressure.

## Product Purpose

This project turns long PROYA livestream VODs into short vertical commerce clips through a local Python pipeline. The dashboard exists to make that complex workflow controllable: operators should quickly understand what is running, what is waiting, what needs review, what failed, and which clips or modules are ready for downstream export or reuse.

Success means the team can keep the pipeline moving with confidence, recover from queue problems quickly, and make high-volume review decisions without digging through logs as the default experience.

## Brand Personality

Composed, legible, trustworthy, and efficient. The product should feel like a production control room for a commercial media workflow: calm under load, clear about state, and respectful of an operator's attention.

## Anti-references

Avoid flashy SaaS marketing patterns, toy-like controls, excessive decoration, decorative gradients, hero-scale type inside operational screens, dense technical log walls as the primary experience, and status colors that require memorization. The dashboard should not feel like a generic analytics template or a playful creator tool.

## Design Principles

1. Surface the next operational decision first.
2. Make every important status readable through text, icon or shape, color, and hierarchy.
3. Keep density useful, not intimidating.
4. Treat technical detail as drill-down context, not the default view.
5. Preserve operator confidence with stable layouts, clear feedback, and predictable controls.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA. Support keyboard-friendly navigation, visible focus states, reduced-motion preferences, color-blind-safe status indicators, readable chart labels and tooltips, and touch targets around 44px minimum on mobile. Color must never carry status alone.
